How It Works

3 Steps. Fully Automated.

Your AI assistant runs the entire lead process from hello to booked appointment — without you touching a thing.

01

Visitor Opens the Chat

Your AI widget greets them warmly and asks what's going on with their roof — storm damage, replacement, repair, or just an inspection.

02

AI Qualifies the Lead

Asks location, job type, urgency, and insurance status. Scores the lead in real time. Decides: qualified for booking, or redirect with email capture.

03

Books or Captures

Qualified leads pick an inspection time directly in chat. Unqualified leads get a polite message and their email is stored for future follow-up.

✓ Qualified Lead

They book an inspection

The right lead in the right service area, with the right job — gets booked automatically.

  • Calendly opens inside the chat
  • They pick a time that works for them
  • Calendar invite sent to both parties
  • Your sales agent gets an instant notification
→ Not Qualified

Email captured for follow-up

Wrong area or wrong job type? The AI handles it gracefully and still captures the contact.

  • Politely informed why they're not a fit
  • AI asks for their email — no pressure
  • Saved to your Google Sheet with reason noted
  • Your team reviews weekly for future outreach
